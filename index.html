About Our Library

The Jabalpur Engineering College Library is a hub of knowledge and resources for students, faculty, and researchers. Established in 1947, our library has grown to house over 100,000 books, journals, and digital resources.

Our mission is to support the academic and research needs of the JEC community by providing access to quality information resources and services.
